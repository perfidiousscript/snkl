About

SNKL (pronounced Cenacle) is an interactive, user modifiable visualization of authors of the 20th century.

Existing book suggestion algorithms are opaque and sometimes dead wrong

SNKL seeks to make the connections between authors apparent, and the process of finding new works more efficient.

This allows the reader to start from writers that they have previously enjoyed, select the type of connection that interests them, and search from there

Each writer is displayed on a graph of time and relative style.

The lines display the span of their like, the box spans the time between their first and last major works.

Clicking on a author's box displays color coded lines to connected authors.

Clicking on a writer will display more information about them.

Written in Node/Exress, AngularJS and d3.js on a Post-GreSQL database by Sam Moss.
